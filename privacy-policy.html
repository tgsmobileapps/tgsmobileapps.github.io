Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2025

TGS Mobile Apps ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Authenticator App MFA 2FA mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information on our servers. All 2FA account data (secrets, usernames, service names) is stored locally on your device only.

Camera Usage

The App requests access to your device camera solely to scan QR codes for adding 2FA accounts. Camera data is not stored, transmitted, or shared.

Biometric Data

The App may use Face ID or Touch ID for authentication. Biometric data is processed entirely by your device's secure enclave and is never accessed or stored by us.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the App. This data does not include any personally identifiable information.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

Data Security

All 2FA secrets are stored locally on your device. We recommend enabling biometric lock for additional security.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
