Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a 2FA account?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen, then scan the QR code provided by the service you want to protect, or enter the secret key manually.

How do I back up my accounts?

Go to Settings → Backup & Restore → Export. This feature requires a Premium subscription. Save the exported file in a secure location.

How do I restore my accounts?

Go to Settings → Backup & Restore → Import, then select your previously exported backup file.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All your 2FA account data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store your authentication data on any server.

How do I cancel my subscription?

On iPhone/iPad: Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, find Authenticator App, and tap Cancel Subscription. On Android: Open Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.

What are the subscription options?

Weekly: $1.49 for the first week, then $9.99/week, billed automatically.
Yearly: 7-day free trial, then $49.99/year, billed annually.
Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

What happens to my codes if I delete the app?

Your 2FA codes will be lost if you delete the app without a backup. We strongly recommend exporting a backup before uninstalling.

I lost access to my 2FA codes. What should I do?

If you have a backup file, use Settings → Backup & Restore → Import to recover your codes. If you don't have a backup, contact the individual services directly to regain account access through their account recovery process.
